# Reference Log

## 1. DesignRush 2026 Website Design Awards
- URL: https://www.designrush.com/best-designs/awards/websites
- Source family: DesignRush
- Why relevant: Current enterprise-oriented award archive with strong examples of section ordering, module framing, and controlled UI emphasis rather than pure marketing spectacle.
